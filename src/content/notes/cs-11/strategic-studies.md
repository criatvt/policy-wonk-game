# The Lost Meaning of Strategy

The "lost meaning of strategy" critique argues that modern statecraft has reduced strategy to logistics, planning, and measurable performance metrics — losing the substantive political dimension that gave the discipline its meaning. The result is bureaucratic activity that looks strategic without serving any coherent strategic purpose.

The mechanism is structural. Modern bureaucracies measure what is measurable: budgets, deployments, force levels, exercise frequencies, capability acquisitions, milestones met. Each of these is a *means* indicator. The much harder question — whether these means actually serve the political ends they are nominally aimed at — gets crowded out because it is harder to measure and harder to settle authoritatively. Over time, strategy degrades into the management of measurable activity, regardless of whether the activity adds up to political purpose.

Short-term political incentives compound the degradation. Politicians are evaluated on what they have done in their term in office, not on whether their actions cumulatively served the country's strategic position decades later. The political incentive is to produce visible activity, not to invest in long-term strategic positions. Bureaucracies that align with this incentive produce more activity and less strategy.

Risk aversion adds another layer. Genuinely strategic choices — about alliances, capabilities, areas of focus — involve betting on uncertain futures. Risk-averse institutions avoid these bets and substitute defensible managerial activity. The result is institutional behaviour that looks strategic but is structurally unable to make strategic choices.

## Why this matters in policy

Recovering strategic thinking against the degradation requires deliberate institutional effort. Designated strategic-review processes, civil-military dialogue that pushes back against pure operational thinking, and political leadership that articulates and defends political objectives can all help. Without these, the bureaucratic default is degraded strategy.
